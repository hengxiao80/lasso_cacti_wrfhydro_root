NCAR-RAL Surface Hydrometeorological Observation Network Data
https://data.eol.ucar.edu/dataset/553.034

Summary
This data set contains the 15 minute resolution surface meteorology and soils
data from the 15 NCAR/RAL weather stations that were operated around central
Argentina during the RELAMPAGO (Remote sensing of Electrification, Lightning,
And Meso-scale/micro-scale Processes with Adaptive Ground Observations)
Extended Observing Period (EOP).
